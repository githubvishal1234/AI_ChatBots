CORtracker – Company Overview

CORtracker (also known as CORtracker360) is an enterprise software company that provides integrated business solutions to automate and optimize organizational operations. The company focuses on digital transformation using ERP, CRM, analytics, and automation technologies.

CORtracker offers a modular ERP platform that includes finance, human resources, procurement, inventory management, production, maintenance, and accounting modules. The system is designed to improve cross-department coordination, reduce manual work, and support business scalability.

The CRM solutions provided by CORtracker include lead management, sales automation, customer support, contact management, marketing automation, and analytics. These CRM tools are fully integrated with ERP systems to enable seamless business operations.

CORtracker also provides supply chain management solutions that offer real-time visibility across procurement, inventory, and order fulfillment. The platform helps organizations enhance supply chain efficiency and decision-making through analytics and reporting dashboards.

The company supports both cloud-based and on-premise deployments. Key platform features include customizable integrations, real-time tracking, modular design, and multi-layered security with encryption and role-based access controls.

CORtracker offers custom software development services, including enterprise applications, industry-specific solutions, and web and mobile application development. The company emphasizes scalable architecture and modern engineering practices.

CORtracker focuses on innovation using artificial intelligence, data analytics, automation, and emerging technologies such as IoT and big data analytics. The platform supports predictive analytics and forecasting for data-driven decision-making.

CORtracker has a global presence with clients across multiple continents. The company reports serving over 50 clients worldwide and employs more than 100 professionals across different regions.

The company headquarters is based in Michigan, USA, with operational presence in locations such as Farmington Hills and Novi. CORtracker operates as a global enterprise software and ERP solutions provider.

CORtracker follows a structured work process that includes understanding client workflows, delivering customized and cost-effective solutions, quality assurance, documentation, and post-launch support.

CORtracker IT Private Limited – India Operations

Cortracker IT Private Limited is an Indian subsidiary operating as an IT consulting and support services company. The company was incorporated on 9 October 2019 and is located in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana, India.

The company operates as an active, unlisted private limited entity with a focus on IT consulting, ERP support, and software services. Its registered office is located in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad.

The leadership team includes Vikram Sagar Pasala, Sunitha Pasala, and Madanu Balaswamy. Financial filings indicate variable revenue and profitability trends, with records available up to the financial year 2023.

Employee Reviews and Work Culture
